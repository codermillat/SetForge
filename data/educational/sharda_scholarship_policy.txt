# Context: Specific scholarship details for students from Bangladesh.

## Eligibility Criteria
- The student must have completed their previous qualification (HSC/Diploma/Bachelors) from a recognized Education Board in Bangladesh.
- The scholarship is awarded based on the aggregate CGPA from the previous qualification.

## Scholarship Tiers (Based on CGPA out of 5.0)
- **CGPA 3.5 - 5.0:** 50% scholarship on tuition fee.
- **CGPA 3.0 - 3.4:** 20% scholarship on tuition fee.
- **Result below CGPA 3.0:** No scholarship is generally awarded based on merit.

## Program-Specific Scholarship Rules
- **50% Scholarship Programs (for CGPA 3.5+):** This includes most B.Tech, BBA, MBA, BCA, B.Com, Law, Design, and Paramedical (B.Sc.) programs.
- **B.Sc. Nursing:** A fixed 25% scholarship is awarded annually.
- **Other Programs:** Most other programs not on the 50% list are eligible for a 20% scholarship.
- **NO SCHOLARSHIP Programs:** Scholarships are NOT available for MBBS, BDS, MPT, M.Sc. Nursing, and all Pharmacy programs (B.Pharm, D.Pharm, M.Pharm).

## General Conditions
- **Applicability:** The scholarship applies ONLY to the tuition fee component. It does not cover admission fees, hostel fees, or examination fees.
- **Continuation:** To maintain the scholarship in subsequent years (from 2nd year onwards), the student must pass all exams without any backlogs/fails and maintain a minimum of 75% attendance.
- **Single Scholarship:** A student can only avail one scholarship per year.
